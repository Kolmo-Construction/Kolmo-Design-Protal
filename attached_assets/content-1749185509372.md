## Project Scope: Retaining Walls, Stairs, and Hardscape Features

> **Note:** All materials to be supplied by the customer. This scope covers **labor only**.

### Retaining Walls

Construction of four retaining walls to support grading and landscape transitions:

- **Wall 1**: 26 linear feet
- **Wall 2**: 25 linear feet
- **Wall 3**: 35 linear feet
- **Wall 4**: 5 linear feet

Each wall will include appropriate base preparation and structural construction in accordance with local code.

### Stairs with Railing

- Installation of an 18 ft stair run integrated with the retaining wall layout
- Includes construction of treads/risers and installation of **code-compliant railing**

### Post Wrapping

- Wrap **4 structural posts** using **2"x2" lumber** to provide a clean, finished appearance and added protection

### Paver Edging

- **100 linear feet** of paver edging
- Ensures proper containment and visual definition of paved surfaces
